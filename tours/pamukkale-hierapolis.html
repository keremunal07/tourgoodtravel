Pamukkale Hierapolis Tour

Pamukkale Day Tour from Antalya promises everything one can expect from a cultural tour. Combining both ancient scenery and exotic nature, this city will exceed all of your expectations.

Our Outdoor activity in Antalya will start with us picking you up from your resort via our private vehicle in the morning. The ride will take approximately 3 hours and the tour guide will inform you about the Pamukkale excursion in detail in the vehicle. Around an hour later, our first stop will be Korkuteli, where we will have a tasty Turkish breakfast before continuing. You will also be able to stack up for snacks and drinks for your ride here.

After arriving at Denizli, the place Pamukkale is located in, we will stop by a local restaurant and eat the second meal of our day to be fully prepared for the rest of the trip. As things to do in Pamukkale are endless, we will explore Pamukkale Mountain later.

Our official tour will start with visiting the Ancient City of Hierapolis. At the top of the Pamukkale Mountain, the ancient city has been preserved almost like it was thousands of years ago and we will have the luxury of walking in the same streets the citizens of Hierapolis walked in to fully understand the importance of this old city. The Roman columns, altars, temples and other architectural structures will make you feel like you're right there with your old ancestors. Our guide will tell you all about this UNESCO listed world heritage and answer all of your questions.

Then, we will get to walk through the mountain and visit the famous travertines. The white natural wonders are filled with warm thermal water and the unique shapes they possess make them incredible picture material. Also you'll be able to swim in the ancient Cleopatra Pool via paying the fee.

After we explore everything Pamukkale has to offer, we will drive back to Pamukkale for shopping & a delicious dinner. For our Pamukkale Excursion from Antalya, we will visit a wine facility and a carpet workshop, both incredibly popular and well known in Denizli. After visiting these local places, we will go to a famous Turkish restaurant for dinner. Last but not least for the Antalya Daily Tour, we will stop in Antalya for a short break and enjoy the mesmerizing scenery while the sun sets. Then, we will drop you back off at your resort and our Culture Excursion in Antalya will come to an end.
