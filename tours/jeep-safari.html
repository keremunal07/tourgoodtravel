Jeep Safari Tour

Our Jeep Safari Excursion in Antalya will begin with us picking you up from your resort via our private vehicle at the arranged time. We will drive to the Safari area which is excluded from urban life and hidden with grooves all around it. When we arrive, the instructors will welcome you and inform you about the route and safety equipment. The route is approximately 140 kilometers long and the 4•4 Jeep can take up to 8 people in. We recommend this Outdoor Activity in Antalya to big families who want to make unforgettable memories with their loved ones during vacation.

After everyone gets in, the ride will start. We will pass by colorful Turkish villages that are great picture and video material. We will stop by Yumaklar Village, a place where humble residents will welcome you and make sure you explore this exceptional area standing high at 920 meters. Then, we will go to the village’s local restaurant to make sure we're energetic enough to continue our route. The delicious Turkish meals will be served and we will have plenty of time to eat and digest.

When we hit the road again, we will drive through Taurus Mountains, the giant trees that are home to chirping birds and muddy roads will accompany us through the place.

Our next stop for the Daily Tour In Antalya will be Taurus Mountains. The high waterfall that also consists of a natural pool will leave you in awe and you'll be able to swim in the pool, explore the area and interact with the wildlife. The jeeps are fast and the route is harsh, so this break will make sure you get your energy back and relax before continuing the adrenaline induced tour.

After our time comes to an end at Ucansu Waterfall, our Adventure Tour in Antalya will continue fast and non-stop, and we will be on our way back. When we get back to the starting point, we will have time to take a look at pictures and videos taken by the professional staff. After we make sure we have everything we need to never forget this Antalya Safari Excursion, we will get in our private vehicle and drop you back off at your resort and our excursion will come to an end.
